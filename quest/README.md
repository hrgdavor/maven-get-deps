# ⚔️ The Chronicles of Dependency: A Hero's Journey

Welcome, Traveler! You stand at the threshold of the **Telekinetic Nexus**. Here, you will find the ancient scrolls necessary to master the arts of **Thin Deployments** and **System Empowerment**. As your mastery grows, you shall manifest a **Stable Resolution Aura**—a passive phenomenon known to cause misbehaving software to suddenly cooperate the moment you walk into the room to help a distressed colleague.

Each chronicle offers unique loot (knowledge) and challenges. Choose your path wisely! (just kidding, ATM you need to go through quests in order).

---

### 🔨 [Quest 1: The Java Forge](./java-forge.md)
*Crafting Arcane JARs*

Master the core rituals of Maven multi-module builds and dependency extraction. Learn to forge thin artifacts that are as light as air but as strong as steel.
- **Loot**: Maven Mastery, Thin JAR Crafting.

---

### 🕯️ [Quest 2: The Sentinel's Ritual](./sentinel-ritual.md)
*Conjuring the Systemd Spirits*

Learn to bind your applications to the realm forever as Systemd Daemons. Master the secondary arts of User Isolation, Group Permissions, and ACL Warding.
- **Loot**: Daemonization, Security Warding, Permission Alchemy.

---

### 🌀 [Quest 3: The Chrono-Shifter's Leap](./chrono-shifter.md)
*Masters of the Symlink Swap*

Master the forbidden arts of Version Management and Atomic Swapping. Learn to leap between versions in an instant and use the "Developer's Blink" (rsync) for rapid transport.
- **Loot**: Atomic Deployment, CI Interfacing, Rsync Transport.

---

> [!TIP]
> All quests use the **[Multi-Module Example](../examples/multi)** as the practice ground. Ensure you have explored its ruins before beginning.

May your builds be fast and your dependencies secure!
